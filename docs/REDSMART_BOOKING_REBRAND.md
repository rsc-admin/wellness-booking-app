# RedSmart Booking Rebrand

## Positioning
RedSmart Booking is a booking service for providers, companies, and individuals that need to publish availability, accept customer bookings, manage provider schedules, and track lightweight service operations.

The product is app-agnostic by design: it can support providers, field service companies, consultants, instructors, local service businesses, and other appointment-based operators.

## Responsibility Notice
RedSmart Booking provides booking and scheduling software only. Providers, companies, and individuals using the service remain solely responsible for the services they offer and deliver, including licensing, credentials, insurance, pricing, taxes, customer communications, cancellations, safety, quality, and outcomes.

This notice should appear in production terms, provider onboarding materials, customer booking flows, and provider agreements.
